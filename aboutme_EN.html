About me

Hello! My name is Die (Diego in official documents) Hernández-Prieto. I am currently working at the Laboratorio de Fitoquímica de Alimentos Saludables (LabFAS), at the Centro de Edafología y Biología Aplicada del Segura, in Murcia, Spain, as a postdoctoral researcher.

I studied Biology at the Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, where I designed an implementation to couple the Gillespie algorithm and the Brownian motion algorithm to simulate gene expression associated with the movement of a transcription factor. At the University of Murcia I did a Master's degree in Bioinformatics, with a Master's thesis in which, based on microarray analysis, I determined the virulence of the genetic networks associated with the flagellum of E.coli.

My PhD, obtained in 2024 from the Polytechnic University of Cartagena, and my current research focuses on the computational modelling of different biological systems associated with experiments in food science, carried out in the field of natural vegetable beverages.
