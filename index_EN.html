Healthy Foods Phytochemistry Lab

New healthy food (fresh/processed). Bioactive phytochemicals.

Who are we?

We are a research group focused on the study and viability of healthy plant-based foods.

Where are we?

We are at the Centro de Edafología y Biología Aplicada del Segura (CEBAS), belonging to the Consejo Nacional de Investigaciones Científicas (CSIC), in Murcia, Spain.

Work with us!

We always welcome students and collaborations. Contact us at mail@mail.com

Our services

Food analysis

Chronic and acute studies in volunteers, analyzing urine and plasma. Targeted and non-targeted metabolomics.

Recipe design

Specialty citrus (lemon) and berry beverages with antioxidant properties.

Processing optimization

Pasteurization, microbial inactivation, high pressures.

Bioinformatics Service

Massive data analysis, predictive machine learning models, automatic search for markers in the metabolome.
